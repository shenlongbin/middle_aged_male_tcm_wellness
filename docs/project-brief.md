## Why This Project Exists

Educated 75s/80s generation users are entering a stage of rising health anxiety, declining energy, poor sleep, metabolic warning signs, and fear of aging. They do not respond well to traditional elderly marketing or low-trust保健品话术, but they are open to structured, dignified, logic-driven health guidance.

## Target Market

Primary market: Mainland China.

Secondary expansion market: overseas Chinese and international wellness audiences after domestic validation.

## Core Opportunity

Occupy the "scientific wellness explainer" position for middle-aged men who respect modern medicine but also want a broader, more actionable long-term conditioning framework.

## Business Model Direction

Start with content and trust.
Then expand into memberships, courses, habit programs, curated products, and enterprise or community health workshops.

## Constraints

- Solo or small-team execution
- Low initial capital
- Need fast validation
- Must avoid medical overclaim and pseudo-scientific framing

## Immediate Goal

Define a focused MVP content product and its first acquisition path.
