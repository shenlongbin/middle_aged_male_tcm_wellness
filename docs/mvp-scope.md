# MVP Scope

## Core Product Statement

Build a lightweight Chinese content site for educated middle-aged men that explains sub-health and TCM wellness in modern language and captures early audience trust and intent.

## MVP Goal

Validate whether the target audience resonates strongly enough with the positioning to consume content, return, and opt into a follow-up channel.

## In Scope

- One landing homepage
- Clear brand positioning
- Three pillar topic sections
- Three detailed content pages or page sections
- One lead capture or waitlist form
- One feedback path
- Basic analytics hooks

## Out of Scope

- Medical diagnosis
- Personalized prescription recommendations
- Full course platform
- Community product with accounts
- E-commerce checkout
- Native app
- AI chatbot with health advice

## Top Value Propositions

1. Explain middle-aged decline in a way that feels intelligent and familiar.
2. Bridge science-minded thinking with TCM-informed wellness.
3. Offer practical first steps without shame, hype, or mystical language.

## Non-Goals

- Serving all age groups
- Serving women in the initial version
- Competing with hospitals or medical apps
- Covering every TCM topic
- Building a broad wellness marketplace
