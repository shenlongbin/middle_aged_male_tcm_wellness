## Core User Flow

1. Entry from social post, search result, or shared article
2. Immediate recognition through headline and hero copy
3. Scroll through "you are not imagining it" problem framing
4. See structured explanations for common middle-aged decline patterns
5. Read one deeper topic section or article
6. Join waitlist or request updates

## Key Screen Definitions

### Homepage Hero

- Strong audience callout
- Message that this is not low-trust elderly marketing
- Promise of modern, structured, TCM-aware wellness guidance

### Problem Pattern Section

- Show clusters such as poor sleep, belly fat, fatigue,脂肪肝,血脂, libido decline, stress
- Emphasize these often come together, not separately

### Framework Section

- Explain the project's perspective:
  modern medicine for disease and risk detection
  wellness and habit systems for long-term conditioning
  TCM as an interpretation and routine framework, not magic

### Topic Pillars

- Sleep and recovery
- Metabolic warning signs
- Stress, anxiety, and aging identity
- TCM concepts translated

### Conversion Section

- Invite users to get a starter plan or future updates
- Ask a simple interest question rather than a medical intake form

## Critical States

- Empty state: no email submitted yet
- Success state: waitlist confirmation
- Error state: form submission failed
- Mobile state: all sections remain readable with short paragraphs
